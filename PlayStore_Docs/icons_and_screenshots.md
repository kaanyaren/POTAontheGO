# Icons & Screenshots Guidelines

Follow Google's asset specifications exactly to avoid rejection.

## App Icons

- Size: 512x512 px, PNG, transparent background
- Filename: `ic_launcher_playstore.png`

## Feature Graphic

- Size: 1024x500 px, JPG or PNG
- No transparency
- Used on store listing header

## Screenshots

- Minimum of 2 screenshots per supported device type
- Phone: 1080x1920 or similar aspect ratio
- Tablet: 1920x1080 or 2048x1536
- Provide landscape and portrait as needed
- Format: PNG or JPEG
- Filenames: `screenshot_1.png`, `screenshot_2.png`, ...

## Promotional Graphics

- Optional: TV banner (1280x720), promo video, etc.

## Notes

- Place all assets in `PlayStore_Docs/assets/` before uploading.
- Keep source design files in `docs/` or `assets/` repo folder for versioning.
